A Note from the Author

I've always been passionate about stories that blend elite glamour with deep human dramas, like Gossip Girl and The Vampire Diaries. However, I noticed that many of these captivating plots didn't address themes of faith, forgiveness, and redemption in a meaningful way, or even featured toxic relationships.

Welcome to New York: Born Ricciwood was born from the desire to create a tribute to these beloved series, but with a different voice. It's an answer to the search for a romance that, beyond intrigue and luxurious settings, explores personal growth, healing wounds, and the beauty of genuine new beginnings. My inspiration was to bring a stunning New York, complex characters, and a love that, even amidst chaos, finds a greater purpose.
